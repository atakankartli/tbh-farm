Manual dungeon switching from the dashboard.

- **Send the game to any dungeon from the web GUI**: a green play button on every chest card, plus a "Go now" button in the Add Dungeon picker (works for all 108 stages — it doesn't have to be a farm target). The macro aborts whatever it's waiting on and navigates immediately, so the script and the game never disagree about where you are. Works even while the macro pill is paused, since it's an explicit command from you. Nothing is stamped and your targets are untouched.

Everything else as in v1.3.4 (editable chest levels, log-only drop watching, macro/stash off by default).
